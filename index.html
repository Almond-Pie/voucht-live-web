Why Voucht exists

Built for the businesses Google ignores

When someone searches "coffee near me" or "best barber in Peckham," Google decides who shows up first. The ranking comes down to one thing more than any other: how many recent reviews you have. The businesses at the top get the walk-ins. The ones at the bottom get scrolled past.

Most independent businesses have 10–20 reviews. The ones with 50+ consistently outperform their neighbours. But café owners, barbers, and restaurant managers don't have the time or the tools to build a review system — they're too busy actually running the business.

That's where we come in. Voucht was built by people who've worked behind the counter, who know what it's like to serve great coffee while your competitor with worse coffee but more reviews gets all the Google traffic. We built the system we wished existed.
